Services

We use interventions and procedures that have been empirically validated within the science of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA). These principles consists of using the most up to dat procedures, which are consistent with what has been demonstrated to work within the research of ABA. The first line of support consist of attempting to use positive reinforcement, while making needed adjustments.

Every intervention is tailored for each person. Our clinical team ensures that the procedures and intervention used, meet the needs of the person. We are all different, so this holds true for every person we serve.

How do we know something is working?

We measure it! We document to know whether progress is being made across the individual goals and objectives. This information is consistently presented to caregivers and funding sources, so it is possible to know how much progress has been made.

Parent's Role

At ICAN-B we encourage parents to be active participants in our programs.
During sessions, opportunities are placed for parents to participate hands on with their child. Through these opportunities we strive for parents to have a understanding on managing behavior in the absence of our team.
